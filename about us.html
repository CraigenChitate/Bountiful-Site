Who we are?

We are a small business specializing in, locally grown foods. We are located in Benoni, Johannesburg, however, we have trucks that cover most areas in South Africa. We sell fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, we deliver and manage orders on the phone and via the website.

Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the people of South Africa.
